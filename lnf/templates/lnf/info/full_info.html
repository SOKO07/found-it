Our Vision

Found IT envisions a future where owners instantly find their belongings without effort. Our goal is to inspire other institutions to integrate our system within their boundaries to ensure the certainty of recovering items.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a reliable and user-friendly website that assists users in recovering their items efficiently. We attempt to provide an innovative approach to handling lost items, ensuring users can regain them quickly. At Found It, we believe that technology can reshape the standard of lost and found methods.
